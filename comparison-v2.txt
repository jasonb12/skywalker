DETAILED COMPARISON: Our Map vs Skyway.run
===========================================

CRITICAL DIFFERENCES:

1. LINE THICKNESS: Skyway.run uses VERY thick lines (about 12-15px equivalent). Our lines are 6px at zoom 15 - need to at least double.

2. LINE STYLE: Skyway.run uses a distinctive "X" pattern on the lines, not simple dashes. The lines have circular nodes/dots at intersections and X marks along the path. This is a custom MapLibre style, not standard Leaflet line styles.

3. COVERAGE: Skyway.run shows paths extending much further:
   - East to US Bank Stadium (far right)
   - West to Target Field (far left)  
   - South to Loring Parking Ramp
   - North to The Churchill
   Our map is missing the eastern paths (US Bank Stadium area), some western paths, and some southern paths.

4. COLORS: Skyway.run has very vivid, saturated colors. Our colors are similar but less prominent because lines are thin.

5. BUILDING LABELS: Skyway.run shows many more building labels, positioned more carefully.

6. The paths in skyway.run follow the EXACT building outlines and streets. Our paths are in the right general area but may be slightly offset.

ACTION PLAN:
- Increase line weight to 12+ at zoom 15
- Need to download MORE tiles to get the eastern (US Bank Stadium) and western (Target Field) paths
- The X pattern is unique to MapLibre vector rendering - in Leaflet we can approximate with thicker dashed lines or use a custom pattern
