Minneapolis Skyway Map Analysis - Accurate Building Positions and Connections
=============================================================================

KEY OBSERVATIONS FROM OFFICIAL MAPS:
1. The Minneapolis street grid is rotated ~15 degrees clockwise from true north
2. Streets run NW-SE (numbered streets) and NE-SW (avenues)
3. The skyway system follows this rotated grid, NOT a perfect N-S/E-W grid
4. Skyway bridges cross streets diagonally and at angles
5. Nicollet Mall runs NW-SE through the center (the pink/magenta line on maps)

STREET GRID (from NW to SE):
- Hennepin Ave runs NE-SW on the west side
- 1st Ave N, then Nicollet Mall, then Marquette Ave, then 2nd Ave S, 3rd Ave S, etc.
- Streets run NW-SE: Washington Ave, 1st St, 2nd St, 3rd St... up to 10th St

SKYWAY CONNECTIONS (bridges between buildings):
The skyway bridges cross streets at various angles. Key connection patterns:
- Along Nicollet Mall (NW-SE): buildings connected in a chain
- Along Marquette Ave: parallel chain
- Cross-connections between Nicollet and Marquette chains
- Eastern extensions to Government Center, US Bank Stadium area
- Western extensions to Target Center, LaSalle area
