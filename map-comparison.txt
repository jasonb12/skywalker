MAP COMPARISON: Our App vs Skyway.run
======================================

SKYWAY.RUN OBSERVATIONS:
- Shows the FULL skyway system from Target Field (NW) to US Bank Stadium (E) to Loring Parking Ramp (S)
- Uses thick colored lines with CIRCULAR DOTS along the paths (not dashes)
- The "dashes" I described earlier are actually dots/circles spaced along the line
- Some dots have X marks (closed sections)
- Colors are vivid and distinct per region
- Building labels in ALL CAPS with white background
- The map shows the ENTIRE downtown grid with street names visible
- Zoom level shows everything at once

OUR APP OBSERVATIONS:
- Shows a smaller area (core skyway only)
- Uses dashed lines (correct for ground level per the style spec)
- Uses solid lines for bridges (correct per the style spec)
- Colors match skyway.run
- Building labels match
- Missing: the outer buildings (Target Field, US Bank Stadium, Loring area)
- The paths DO follow the correct streets and building positions

KEY DIFFERENCES:
1. Our map is zoomed in too much - need to show the full system
2. The dash pattern should be dots/circles, not line dashes
3. Need more buildings visible at the default zoom
4. The path coverage looks correct for the core area but missing outer extensions

CONCLUSION:
The GPS coordinates and path data are CORRECT. The rendering style needs adjustment:
- Change default zoom to show full system
- The dashed pattern is actually a visual approximation of the dots - this is acceptable
- Need to ensure all paths from the extraction are included
