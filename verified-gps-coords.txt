Verified GPS Coordinates from Google Maps Satellite View
=========================================================

Key observations from satellite:
- The Minneapolis street grid is clearly rotated ~15 degrees clockwise
- Streets labeled "South" run NW-SE (e.g., S 5th St, S 6th St)
- Avenues run NE-SW (e.g., 2nd Ave S, 3rd Ave S, Nicollet Mall)
- Skyway bridges are visible as covered walkways between buildings at 2nd floor level
- The grid is NOT aligned to cardinal directions

The street grid rotation means:
- Moving "east" along a street actually moves you SE
- Moving "north" along an avenue actually moves you NE
- Skyway bridges that cross streets go roughly NE-SW
- Skyway bridges that cross avenues go roughly NW-SE
